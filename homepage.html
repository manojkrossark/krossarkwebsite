    Alto.park

    Alto.park AI is the next-generation, AI-driven parking platform purpose-built for Southeast Asia’s fast-evolving urban ecosystems. alto.park empowers smarter, safer, and greener parking.

    Urban Parking Challenge

    • Wasted Time: Up to 30 minutes per trip spent searching for parking in dense city areas like Marina Bay (Singapore) or Bukit Bintang (Kuala Lumpur).
    • Traffic Congestion: 25–30% of city traffic in areas like Orchard Road is caused by vehicles circling for parking.
    • Underutilized Private Spots: Residential and commercial lots lie idle—even during peak hours.
    • EV Growth Blockers: Users avoid EVs due to lack of real-time charger visibility and pre-booking.
    • Disconnected Solutions: Current apps don’t account for safety, weather, events, or offer real-time verified visibility.
    Alto.ev Problems

    Range Anxiety: Drivers often doubt their battery range to the next charging station.

    Lack of Real-Time Charging Station Info: Maps/apps often miss real-time charging station availability.

    Poor Integration with Driving Conditions: Navigation systems overlook holistic EV route optimization.

    Fragmented Experience for Hybrid Vehicles: Hybrid users lack a unified solution that seamlessly integrates electric charging and petrol refueling.

    Inefficient Dashboard Solutions: Android dashboards lack smart, real-time widgets for charge monitoring and adaptive route suggestions.

    Alto.ev Solution

    • Smart EV Routing with weather + traffic + battery status
    • Live Charging Station Recommendations (availability, compatibility, wait time)
    • Hybrid Integration for electric + petrol stations in a unified view
    • Android Dashboard Widgets for real-time glanceable info while riding
    • Battery-Aware Routing to minimize charge stops and time
