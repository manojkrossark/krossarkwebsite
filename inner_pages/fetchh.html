FETCHH Last-Mile Delivery Solution for Netmeds

Netmeds, a leading Indian online pharmacy, partnered with Fetchh to optimize last-mile delivery, improve reach in rural areas, and enhance customer satisfaction. This collaboration aims to streamline delivery operations and reduce costs.

Netmeds often faced delays in last-mile deliveries, especially in remote and hard-to-reach areas. This led to customer dissatisfaction and a risk of loss in customer loyalty.

The company was dealing with high delivery costs due to inefficient routing and limited visibility into delivery performance, which impacted overall profitability.

Netmeds relied on multiple logistics partners, leading to fragmented operations and inconsistent service quality.

Customers expected fast and reliable delivery with real-time tracking, which was challenging for Netmeds due to the lack of an integrated delivery solution.

Solution Design with Fetchh

AI-Driven Dynamic Route Optimization

Fetchh implemented a routing engine powered by AI that considered real-time traffic data, weather conditions, and customer location. This feature allowed delivery agents to choose the most efficient routes, significantly reducing delivery times and fuel costs.
