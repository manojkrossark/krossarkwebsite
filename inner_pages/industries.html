Aftermarket and Services

In the aerospace and defense sector, the aftermarket presents one of the most significant but underleveraged sources of long-term value. We help clients unlock this potential by taking a data-driven approach to understanding their installed base — from aircraft systems and defense platforms to mission-critical components.

By identifying where, how, and when parts are used, replaced, or upgraded, we surface high-impact opportunities for recurring revenue, smarter inventory management, and predictive maintenance. Our approach combines pricing optimization, service model transformation, and advanced analytics to drive profitability across the lifecycle.

Whether you're a prime contractor or a Tier 1 supplier, we help you:

  • ✅ Map and segment the installed base to tailor aftermarket strategies
  • ✅ Develop dynamic pricing models that reflect real-time value and demand
  • ✅ Streamline MRO (Maintenance, Repair, Overhaul) operations for efficiency
  • ✅ Transition from transactional sales to service-based models (SaaS, PaaS)
  • ✅ Create digital twins for smarter service planning and logistics

Strategic Innovation in Aerospace & Defense

We help Aerospace and Defense organizations gain mission-critical advantages through next-gen strategy, AI-driven operations, and resilient system design. From enhancing supply chain agility to optimizing mission planning, our solutions are built for speed, scale, and security. Whether you're modernizing legacy systems or accelerating innovation cycles, we bring the tools and insight to help you lead in a high-stakes, rapidly evolving sector.

The aerospace and defense (A&D) industry is facing numerous challenges linked to an unforeseen pandemic, rapidly evolving technologies, and vulnerable supply chains. We help our clients push the boundaries in air and space and safeguard livelihoods through capabilities, technologies, and talent.

Our approach combines a deep understanding of industry dynamics with advanced analytics and an unparalleled network of experts. We work with clients to build internal capabilities, adopt digital and analytics tools, and transform work processes to improve their business in substantial, sustainable ways.
